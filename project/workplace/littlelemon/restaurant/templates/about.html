About Us

Based in Andhra Pradesh, India, Hot N' Fast is a family owned Mediterranean restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Indian, Greek, and Turkish culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Hot N' Fast is owned by two Indian Sisters, Lakshmi and Varshitha, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Lakshmi relies on family recipes and her experience as a chef in India.

Varshitha does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Indian to incorporate additional cuisines from the mediterranean region.
